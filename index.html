INSTRUCTIONS

Welcome to the boltzmannheat
web lab


With the help of this web app, we will explore the conecepts entropy, temperature, heat capacity and thermal equilibrium.

We will learn about the Boltzmann distribution for a system and the effect of heat flow between two systems.

Background

The systems
To your right, two systems are represented: A(left) and B (right). In each you can define the distance between energy levels and plot the Boltzmann distribution at a given temperature that is set either using the slider or the spinner wheel of each system.

Given these system properties, the total molar and entropy per particle will be calculated and shown in the data tab, as will the corresponding total, molar and energy per particle. Also, the partition function q will be calculated and displayed.

In this lab setup, all energy is supplied as heat (no pressure-volumework), i.e the volume V of the systems is constant.
